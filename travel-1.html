    Travel

    Getting to San Francisco

    The closest airport to San Francisco is the San Francisco International Airport (SFO). Other nearby airports are Oakland (OAK) and San Jose (SJC).


    Getting Around San Francisco

    San Francisco is a small city and parking is sparse and difficult. We recommend getting to the ceremony and reception via Lyft and Uber . Ride sharing is cheap and reliable here.

    We also recommend using public transportation when going to tourist destinations. MUNI is the system of buses that travel around San Francisco. BART is a high speed train that runs along Oakland, Berkeley, San Francisco, and San Jose.


    Getting to the Ceremony (St Teresa of Avila Catholic Church)

    The church is located in the corner of Connecticut St and 19th Street. The entrance is on 19th Street.
